About Me

Research

I believe every project should start with user research to understand the users and identify the right problem we need to solve for them. Good research up front can help a product go a long way.

Depending on what I want to learn about the users and what stage the project is at, it is very important to select the right research method. I keep a list of methods in my back pocket and pick the best one when I need it.

Design

I love making products beautiful. But when it comes to design, nothing is more important than the overall experience. I always try to deliver the most delightful user experience to my target audiences.

With my experience with Design Thinking, I take into consideration the needs of people, the possibility of technology, and the requirements of the business. Working closely with different stakeholders drives my design towards success.

Extras

I enjoy coding whenever I get the chance. I'm also taking some Machine Learning and Big Data courses to gain insights into these fields.

These exercises help me gain deeper insights into the possibilities and limitations of various technologies. Even though I'm not an expert in those fields, having the background knowledge and being able to speak their language helps me greatly when working on related projects.
